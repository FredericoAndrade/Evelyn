About

Evelyn Hockstein is an American photographer. Hockstein has taken shots of former United States President Bill Clinton; in 2002 she traveled to Nairobi, Kenya on assignment, and has lived and photographed there since. Hockstein has covered the Hockstein's work has appeared in The New York Times, The New Yorker, TIME Magazine, Newsweek, Stern, L'Express, U.S. News and World Report and ESPN.
